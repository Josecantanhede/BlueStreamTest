Services

Full service mechanical, electric, plumbing and fire suppression contractor providing a wide range of services in the commercial, educational, and hospitality industries with successful installations across the United States and the US Virgin Islands. Our team has expert knowledge in many large scale facilites such as Hotels, Prisons, Universities, School Districs, High Rise Multi-tenant Housing Authorities, and Military Bases. We also have a division that takes care of water conservation, solar, thermal, and other niche technologies.

Ed Foran, Founder

Ed founded the company that would eventually become Bluestream about 18 years ago when he came to Florida from NY. His early career life was fill with experiences in plumbing, gas, and heavy equipment work as an apprentice and eventually a foreman for some of the biggest plumbing companies in the Bronx and Queens. Suffice it to say, that he gained real life knowledge coupled with the toughness and work ethics that comes from working in NY and is using them to lead the extraordinary team that makes Bluestream. With over 30 years of experience under his belt, being a master of trades, Ed brings a wealth of knowledge that is second to none in the industry.
